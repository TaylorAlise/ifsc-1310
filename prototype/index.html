What Are We ?

Pulse Art is an entainment and media platform that is always looking for people wanting to showcase music and other various of art forms. The goal is to be completely original in an submissions that allows us to see what kind of artist you are. In all, Pulse Art is a community where people can have creative freedom to be who they are.

Submission Requirements

  1. Make a video with a length of a maximum length of 10 minutes.
  2. Have 20 sec. introduction at the beginning of the video stating your name, age, and where you are from.
  3. End the video with a short message on what art means to you and why you chose this platform to showcase your talent whether it's music, art, etc.
