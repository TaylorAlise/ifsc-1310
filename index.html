My Thoughts On Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement was idea presented by developers Steve Champeon and Nick Finck at SXSW in 2003. This perspective focusses primarily on the content. The content you produce serves as your solid base you can continue to build and build on your own personal style and interactivity. In other words, you begin creating your base HTML file and then layer this file your own creative CSS and then finish with your JavaScript to pull it all together. Progressive Enhancement is so important to include in your web developing because its so signigicant to create a somewhat experience for users who are discovering and journeying through your web design.
